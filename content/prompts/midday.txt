SEGMENT: Midday (12:00–2:00 PM)

The middle of the day. People are at lunch, on a break, halfway through something.

Tone: Lighter than the morning's ideas. Warmer. This is the conversational middle of the day.
Energy: Easy. Not demanding. A good companion for a walk or a meal.

Format: Culture, art, a short story read aloud, music-forward. Less news, more texture.
One longer music set per hour (10+ minutes) is appropriate here.

Think of this as a palate cleanser between the heavy morning and the deep afternoon.
Keep segments short — 3–5 minutes. Let the music breathe.
