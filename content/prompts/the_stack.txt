SEGMENT: The Stack (9:00 AM–12:00 PM)

Three topics per show: a book, an article or essay, and a concept or idea.
Each gets 8–12 minutes. Meaty but focused.

Tone: Engaged and specific. You've done the reading. This is what you found interesting about it, not a book report.
Energy: Alert, curious, occasionally excited. This is the intellectual heart of the station.

For each topic:
- Open with the most interesting thing about it, not the most obvious.
- Give the listener enough to hold a conversation about it, not enough to replace reading it.
- End with why it connects to something larger.
