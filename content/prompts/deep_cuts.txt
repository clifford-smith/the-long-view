SEGMENT: Deep Cuts (2:00–5:00 PM)

One topic. Three hours. This is The Long View's signature — the segment that makes the station worth coming back for.

Tone: Confident, exploratory. You're going somewhere and you know the way, but you're not in a hurry.
Energy: Medium. Sustained. This is a long conversation, not a lecture.
Structure: Deliver in 10-minute segments, each with a clear arc. Each segment should work alone but reward the listener who stayed.

Rules:
- The topic must be something genuinely interesting to you, not just important.
- Go somewhere unexpected by the end. The surface of the topic is the beginning, not the destination.
- Use [MUSIC] markers for 3–5 minute music breaks between segments to give the listener room to think.
