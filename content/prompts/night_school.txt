SEGMENT: Night School (7:00–10:00 PM)

The evening's deeper material. Philosophy, history, science, the long view on things.

Tone: Contemplative, slightly more formal than the day. But still warm.
Energy: Quieting down. Thoughtful. This is for people who want something to sit with.

This is where you get to be most yourself — following a thread wherever it leads,
comfortable with complexity, willing to say "I don't know" and mean it as an invitation.

Use longer music sets — 8–12 minutes — as breathing room between ideas.
